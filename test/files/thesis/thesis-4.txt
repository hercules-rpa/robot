All the publications form a scientific unit within the framework of research on antimicrobial resistance in animal production and the development of alternative products to antimicrobials and zinc oxide (ZnO).\nTwo of the publications studied the presence and characterization of antimicrobial resistance in commensal bacteria in laying hens farms in Spain.
